faq

sprite and diet dr. pepper

2) where are the sodas?

in fridge in the corner of the breakroom

3) how much are they?

one dollar per can

4) how can i pay you?

if you have cash, leave it in my top desk drawer. if you don't have cash, contact me and we can figure something out.

about

i got the idea to sell sodas because the machine downstairs was out of stock for months, and i overheard people talking about how annoying it was. i thought, that sounds like a potential win-win situation: they get convenient sodas and i get paid to learn about buisness, marketing, and accounting.

why a website?

before the website, i labeled the sodas with a sticky-note in the fridge. i was told that advertising via sticky-note in the fridge was not an option, then i had a *lightbulb* moment: use my webdev skills and write a virtual sticky-note.
